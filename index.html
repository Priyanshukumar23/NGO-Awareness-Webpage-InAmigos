About InAmigos Foundation

Founded in 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities.

We actively work in the fields of education, community welfare, environmental sustainability, and overall social development. Our goal is to create a more equitable society where every individual has access to basic necessities and opportunities for growth.

  • Non-profit organization
  • Focus on empowering the underprivileged
  • Driven by passionate volunteers
